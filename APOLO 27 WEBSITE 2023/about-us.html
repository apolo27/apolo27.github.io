Our Story

Get to know us: The Birth of Apolo 27. From Dreaming to Reality

2019

Making HistoryRepresenting DR in NASA HERC

In 2019, we started with just a few members, and it was a challenging time for us. But we were determined to represent our country for the first time ever in the NASA Human Exploration Rover Challenge, and we did just that.

2020

Learning from ChallengesFirst Award Amid Global Pandemic

In 2020, despite the challenges posed by the pandemic, we persevered and learned valuable lessons that helped us grow, which led to win our first award: "System Safety Award".

2021

Pushing Ourselves to New Heights Adaptation

In 2021, we continued to promote STEM in our country. Also, we focused in making the Rover's design even better. We also successfully raised awareness for STEM in our country.

2022

Inspiring the Next Generation Winning STEM Engagement Award

In 2022, we focused on even more aspects of STEM. This led us to create the STEM Tour, an event where we showcased interactive STEM projects. This year, we won "STEM Engagement Award".

2023

Pushing the BoundariesPRESENT

In 2023, we're still in the process, but our focus remains on making our rover the best one yet. We also believe that STEM can change the world, that's why we are committed to push it even more in our country.

Our Awards

System Safety

In 2020, the team won the System Safety Award, demonstrating that we had the most secure rover in the NASA HERC competition. This was a significant accomplishment as it made us the first team from the Dominican Republic to receive an award from NASA.

STEM Engagement

In 2022, the team won the STEM Engagement award, showcasing our commitment to bringing STEM education to our country's society. We organized a STEM Tour, where we presented hands-on projects related to STEM to the public.

Present

Now, in 2023, we are confident that we will continue to represent our country with pride and contribute to the advancement of STEM education and innovation.

STEM is for EVERYONE

STEM education is crucial to Team Apolo 27's mission, as it provides opportunities for innovation and progress, and should be accessible to everyone regardless of abilities or disabilities. The team is committed to promoting STEM education through events like the STEM Tour and school visits, as they believe in inspiring the next generation of STEM professionals in our country. We strongly advocate for the importance of STEM education and believe it can make a positive impact on society and the world. At Apolo 27, STEM is for everyone, and we are proud to contribute to the advancement of STEM education in our country.
