Our Story

Apolo 27 is born back in 2018 and compete for the first time in NASA Human Exploration Rover Challenge in 2018, hand by hand of our Mentor, Ezequiel Díaz. Moved by passion, aside from seeking the victory in this challenge, our principal mission is to inspire dominican society and show the whole world that our country has a lot of talented young people that have numerous habilities ready to be developed.
